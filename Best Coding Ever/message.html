The Beginning Of Bishop David O. Oyedepo Ministry

  • Born on September 27th, 1954. Studied Architecture and later PhD in Human Development.
  • Born again – Feb. 19, 1969.
  • Received call to ministry – May 2, 1981.
  • Began Faith Liberation Hour – May 29, 1981.
  • First church planted in Kaduna – 1983.
  • Published first books – 1985.
  • Founded Bible Institute – 1986.
  • Moved to Lagos – 1989.
  • Founded Dominion Publishing House – 1992.
