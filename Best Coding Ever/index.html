The Beginning Of Bishop David O. Oyedepo Ministry

  • Born on September 27th, 1954. He began his academic career in Architecture and later went on to obtain a PhD...
  • Born again – Feb. 19, 1969, at 15.
  • Received a called to full time ministry – May 2, 1981 at 26
  • Began his Church ministry and planted the 1st Church in Kaduna – Dec. 11, 1983
  • Founded the Word of Faith Bible Institute – Sep. 1: 1986

Awards and Recognition

  1. Fellow of the Nigerian Academy of Education (FNAE)
  2. Distinguished Award for Excellence in Education – OAU, Ile-Ife (2012).
  3. Honorary Doctor of Divinity – Bethel Graduate School.
  4. National Honour: A road was named after him in Abuja (2008).
