History of Living Faith Church [LFC]

Bishop David O. Oyedepo received a call into the ministry through a heavenly vision, where he saw a lineup of battered, beaten, tattered, deformed and groaning people seeking for escape. He began to sob at the vision. In response to his question, “But why Lord?”, God answered: “But from the beginning it was not so, and now the hour has come to liberate the whole world from all oppression of the devil through the preaching of the word of faith, and I am sending you to undertake this task.” Today, he stands as an Apostle of Faith to the nations, whose ministry has continued to spread across many countries of the world. As an accomplished author, he has written over 100 titles, which are circulated both in hard copy and online stores globally. Many of these works have been translated into major languages including French, Chinese, Spanish, and others. He is the Founder and President of Living Faith Church Worldwide, also known as Winners’ Chapel International, and presently oversees over 21,000 churches across Nigeria and several thousands across six continents of the world. He is the founding and Senior Pastor of Faith Tabernacle, Canaanland, Ota, Nigeria, the international headquarters of the church, reputed to be one of the largest single congregations in the world. As a leader of repute, he has redefined leadership as an evolution of values. He is also spearheading a revolution in education across Nigeria through a network of primary and secondary schools, and the establishment of two universities: Covenant University, Ota, Ogun State, and Landmark University, Omu-Aran, Kwara State, Nigeria.
